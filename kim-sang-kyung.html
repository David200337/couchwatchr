Actor

Kim Sang-kyung

Seoul, South Korea, June 1, 1972

Kim Sang-kyung (born June 1, 1972) is a South Korean actor. He is known for his leading roles in Memories of Murder (2003) and May 18 (2007).

Two of his films directed by Hong Sang-soo, Tale of Cinema (2005) and Ha Ha Ha (2010), screened at the Cannes Film Festival. Kim has also starred in numerous television dramas, notably the period epic King Sejong the Great (2008) and the family comedy What's With This Family (2014).

Actor

Maurice Dean Wint

Leicestershire, England, May 1, 1964

Wint was born in Leicestershire, England, and moved to Canada in 1967 with his family. He began to act in Toronto on stage. He graduated from York University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Drama. One of his most famous roles is Quentin in the cult favorite science fiction horror film Cube. His other notable roles were in Hedwig and the Angry Inch, RoboCop: Prime Directives, Psi Factor and the TekWar films and television series. His theatre credits include Courageous, for which he won a Dora Award, The Real McCoy, Where is Kabuki, Serpent Kills and Titus Andronicus, for which he earned a Dora Award nomination. He was married to the actress Colette Stevenson.
